Safety

  • Washyour hands after handling eggs.
  • Keep countertops clean.
  • Don't let perishable ingredients sit on the counter for too long.
  • Wash your utesils and baking sheets well.
  • Use a hand mittin when handeling pans near the oven.
  • Allow time to cool after baking.
